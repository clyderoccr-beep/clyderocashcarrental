Rental Terms & Conditions

By booking a vehicle, you agree to the following terms:

  1. Rental Period: All rentals are weekly. Extensions must be paid before the current period ends.
  2. Late Fees: $15/hour past the scheduled return time, capped at $200. Late fees begin immediately if you fail to return or extend on time.
  3. Payment: All payments are non-refundable. You authorize CCR to charge your saved payment method for extensions, late fees, and approved charges.
  4. Driver Requirements: Valid driver's license required. You must provide accurate identity and contact information.
  5. Vehicle Condition: You are responsible for all damages, loss of keys, towing, impound fees, traffic violations, and mechanical issues caused by neglect or abuse while in your possession.
  6. Insurance: You are responsible for maintaining insurance coverage during the rental period.
  7. Prohibited Use: No subleasing, racing, off-road use, or illegal activity. Vehicle may only be driven by authorized renter.
  8. GPS Tracking: Vehicles may be equipped with GPS. Location data may be used for recovery, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution.
  9. Termination: CCR reserves the right to terminate the rental and recover the vehicle for non-payment, violation of terms, or fraudulent activity.
  10. Dispute Resolution: All disputes will be resolved under Georgia state law. Chargebacks will be disputed with documented evidence.

CCR Online Payment, Billing & Authorization Agreement

Last Updated: December 2025

By submitting a payment, creating an account, or booking any vehicle on Clydero Cash Car Rental (CCR), you agree to the following terms. This agreement applies to all customers, whether renting, extending a rental, or paying late fees.

1. General Agreement

  • CCR is a private rental service located in Atlanta, GA.
  • All payments made on this website are for vehicle rental, extensions, late fees, or rent-to-own payments.
  • You must provide valid identity, a valid driver‚Äôs license, and accurate contact information.

2. Payment Authorization

You authorize CCR to process payments using any method you choose at checkout, including Debit/Credit Card (Stripe), Apple Pay (Stripe), and PayPal (including PayPal Vault/Billing Agreements when enabled). Your payment method may be securely saved by Stripe or PayPal for future rental extensions or late fees. No card or PayPal data is stored on CCR servers.

3. Saved Payment Methods & Future Charges

a. Stripe (Card / Apple Pay)
Stripe creates a secure token for your card. You authorize CCR to charge this saved method ONLY for approved rental extensions, late fees, outlined damage fees, and mileage overages (if applicable).

b. PayPal Vault / Billing Agreement
If you pay using PayPal, PayPal may create a Billing Agreement/Vault token. CCR may bill that agreement for rental extensions, late fees, and other agreed charges under your rental contract. These charges are limited to those listed in this agreement and CCR policies.

4. Late Fees

Late fee structure: $15.00 per hour past the scheduled return time OR the daily rental rate, whichever is higher. Late fees begin immediately after the scheduled return time unless an extension is approved. You authorize CCR to use your saved Stripe or PayPal payment method for late fees if you return late, fail to communicate, or do not pay an approved extension before the deadline.

5. Rental Extensions

Extensions must be paid before the previous rental period ends. If not paid on time, late fees may apply automatically. Extensions can be paid with any available payment method or saved method.

6. Rent-to-Own Terms

If approved for rent-to-own: You are responsible for insurance, maintenance, and damages. Payments are weekly unless otherwise agreed in writing. Missing payments may result in late fees, recovery of the vehicle, or termination of the agreement. Title transfers only after full payment.

7. Damages & Responsibility

  • All damages while the vehicle is in your possession.
  • Loss of keys.
  • Towing or impound fees.
  • Traffic and parking violations.
  • Mechanical or physical damage caused by neglect or abuse.

8. Refunds

All payments are non-refundable. Once a booking or extension is paid, CCR does not offer refunds.

9. Fraud & Non-Payment

Fraudulent chargebacks will be disputed. GPS data and executed rental agreements may be used as evidence. Non-payment may result in fees, recovery of the vehicle, and civil legal action.
